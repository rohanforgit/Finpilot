# 1. Vision

FinPilot exists to make monthly financial planning effortless.

Instead of forcing users to manually track every transaction or learn complicated budgeting systems, FinPilot combines structured monthly planning with AI-powered expense understanding to help users stay financially organized.

The product should feel less like an accounting software and more like having a smart financial planner sitting beside you every month.

---

# 2. Mission

Help families and salaried individuals confidently manage their monthly finances by:

• Planning income and fixed expenses
• Recording unexpected expenses effortlessly
• Automatically categorizing spending using AI
• Planning for yearly expenses
• Recommending smarter financial decisions

---

# 3. The Problem

Today people generally fall into two categories.

### Category 1

They maintain Excel sheets.

Pros
- Flexible
- Familiar
- Complete control

Cons
- Completely manual
- Difficult to maintain
- No intelligent insights
- No planning assistance

---

### Category 2

They use finance apps.

Pros
- Expense tracking
- Nice dashboards

Cons
- Too complicated
- Requires manual transaction logging
- Too many features
- Mostly focuses on the past

---

FinPilot combines the strengths of both.

---

# 4. What FinPilot Is

FinPilot is an AI-assisted Monthly Financial Planner.

Users create their monthly financial structure once.

Every month that structure is reused.

During the month they simply upload expenses.

AI keeps everything organized and continuously provides recommendations.

---

# 5. What FinPilot Is NOT

FinPilot is NOT

❌ An accounting software

❌ A bookkeeping platform

❌ A stock portfolio tracker

❌ A banking application

❌ A UPI application

❌ A net worth tracker

❌ A cryptocurrency platform

❌ An investment platform

❌ A chatbot with finance attached

Every feature added should support monthly financial planning.
